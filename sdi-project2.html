Project 2

I decided to return to story about dirt biking because I enjoy riding in my spare time.

The string function is a representation of a rider’s inner thoughts to self-motivate. For the Boolean function, I decided to go with a true or false situation if the rider loses control due to traction. The rider will either finish the jump safely or “wipe out”.

The outputs show the rider continuing to hit the jump its highest point then outputs the speed it was reached at.
